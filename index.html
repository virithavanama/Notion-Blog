How to Add Advanced filters in Notion

To add AND/OR filters between columns in Notion: 1. Open your database and select the view you want to filter. 2. Click on the three dots in the top right corner to access the view settings. 3. In the left sidebar, click on "Add a filter." 4. Select the column you want to filter on, and set the conditions for that filter. 5. To add more conditions to the this filter, expand the filter by clicking on the down arrow, click on the 3 dots, Add to advanced filter. 6. Click Add filter and add your AND/OR condition
